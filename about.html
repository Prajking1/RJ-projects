Company Overview

Building Tomorrow's Infrastructure

Delivering excellence in EPC solutions with precision and innovation

RJ Projects Pvt. Ltd. is a leading turnkey contracting company specializing in comprehensive EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) solutions. With expertise spanning Plumbing & Sanitation, Fire Fighting Systems, External and Internal Electrification, Water Treatment Systems, Swimming Pool Systems, HVAC, and Communication Systems, we serve a wide range of sectors including residential and commercial buildings, malls, industries, manufacturing units, railways stations, airports, data centers, hotels, resorts, and hospitals.

With extensive experience in delivering high-quality projects for hospitals, IT parks, and institutional complexes, RJ Projects has successfully executed landmark developments such as AIIMS Raipur, Pragati maidan, the MCD Civic Centre in Delhi, reinforcing our reputation for excellence and reliability across India.
